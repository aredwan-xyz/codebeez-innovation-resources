⏳ The AI Timeline

Key moments that shaped AI

From the Transformer paper to the age of autonomous agents — the milestones that matter.

2017
⚡ Attention Is All You Need
The Transformer architecture is published, laying the foundation for every modern AI model.
2018
📖 BERT Redefines NLP
Bidirectional pre-training sets new benchmarks across all language understanding tasks.
2020
🧠 GPT-3 — Few-Shot Emergence
175B parameters demonstrate that scale unlocks emergent capabilities no one predicted.
2022
💬 ChatGPT Breaks the Internet
100 million users in 2 months. The fastest-growing consumer app in history. AI enters mainstream consciousness.
2023
🦙 Open Source Fights Back
Llama 2 opens the floodgates. Mistral, Qwen, and dozens of open models prove open-source can compete.
2024
🎬 AI Creates Cinema
Sora, Runway Gen-3, and Kling demonstrate photorealistic video generation from text prompts.
2025
🤖 Agents Everywhere
AI moves from answering questions to taking actions. Autonomous coding, research, and workflow agents redefine productivity.
